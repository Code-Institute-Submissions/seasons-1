What we do

about us

We are a UK based fresh produce food service. Aiming to bring people the freshest produce in season at affordable prices. We deliver to anywhere in the UK and only deliver fruit and veg that is in season. We pride ourselves on a strictly farm to box to you policy, so the produce you receive will have been hand-picked days before.

Our Products

We aim to supply you with the amount of fresh produce as you need and your family require. We have different sizes of the box depending on the size of your appetite! We try to operate on a no-waste principle so we can tailor your box to specific needs if possible. We also stock other healthy lifestyle products which can be added to your box. Subscriptions
