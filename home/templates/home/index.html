About Us

What we do

We are a UK based fresh produce food service. Aiming to bring people the freshest produce in season at affordable prices. We deliver to anywhere in the UK and only deliver fruit and veg that is in season. We pride ourselves on a strictly farm to box to you policy, so the produce you receive will have been hand-picked days before.
